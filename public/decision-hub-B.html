Fees within your budget

£44,880/yr boarding · published Oct 2024

What we know

  • Full boarding: £44,880/year (2024–25), published on the school's fees page
  • Weekly boarding available at £41,200/year
  • Fees have risen 6.2% year-on-year over the past three years
  • No bursary amounts publicly disclosed

What this means

Full boarding sits comfortably within your £50k threshold. Weekly boarding gives you a further £3,680 of headroom if finances tighten.

⚠ Tradeoff / watch out

At 6.2% annual rises, fees reach ~£53,700 by Year 3 of a five-year stay. Extras — trips, music, laundry — commonly add 8–12% on top. Ask for a cost-of-attendance estimate before signing anything.

What we don't know

Whether bursaries are available for Year 9 entry and what typical award sizes look like. The WOW scheme is aspirational — no confirmed current availability.

Tour question

"Can you give us a full cost-of-attendance estimate including typical extras, and your policy on fee increases during a child's time at the school?"

Ask: Bursar or admissions
