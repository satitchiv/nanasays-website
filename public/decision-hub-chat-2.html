Fees within your budget

£44,880/yr boarding — within your £50k threshold

▲

What we know

  • Full boarding: £44,880/year (2024–25), published fees page
  • Weekly boarding also available at £41,200/year
  • Fees rising 6.2% year-on-year over 3 years

What this means

Full boarding sits within your £50k threshold. Weekly boarding gives £3,680 more headroom.

⚠ Tradeoff / watch out

At 6.2%/yr, fees reach ~£53,700 by Year 3. Extras — trips, music, laundry — commonly add 8–12% on top. Ask for a cost-of-attendance estimate, not just the published rate.

What we don't know

Whether bursaries are available for Year 9 entry or what typical award sizes are.

"Can you give a full cost-of-attendance estimate including typical extras, and your policy on fee increases during a child's time at the school?"

Ask: Bursar or admissions

How does Epsom compare to Wellington on football?

Short answer

Wellington is materially stronger — 32 teams vs 18, ranked 41st vs 71st nationally.

Wellington's U18A reached the ISFA Cup semifinal in 2023–24. Epsom has a solid programme across age groups but no cup runs in recent data.

⚠ Watch out

That difference costs £5,000/year more in fees. Worth confirming whether your son would realistically compete for a school team before that becomes the deciding factor.
