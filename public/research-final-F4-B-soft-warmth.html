Short answer
Three of four are fine for Maya's anchor; Cheltenham reads sport‑dominant.
High confidence

St Mary's protects unstructured time — chapel rhythm gives the week its pauses, and the studio is open through Sundays. Wycombe's Long Sunday rhythm is the closest match for an art‑anchored girl: less sport‑coded than Cheltenham, with quiet practice time built in.

Cheltenham reads as built around fixtures — Saturday matches culture is strong and parents describe weekends as activity‑coded. Benenden's full‑boarding atmosphere mixes both.
