Variant 3

Comparison data table — analytical, benchmark-ready

Matches the tone of the Financial Health table. Parents who are comparing 3+ schools want columns, not cards. Adds sector benchmarks so a number always has context.
Best for Data-focused parents. Multi-school comparisons. The "honest consultant" voice.
Watch out Less emotional impact. Alumni feel like a spreadsheet row.
Headline
10 sports offered · 4 signature · 4 notable alumni · 4 facilities
UK boarding schools of this scale (600–1000 pupils) typically offer 12–20 sports with 3–6 "signature" sports that the school is known for. Reed's sits at the focused end of the spectrum — depth over breadth, which many parents prefer for a child likely to play seriously.

Reed's has invested recently in cricket specifically (Jarrett Centre, 2018). For a parent whose child is a serious cricketer, this is a strong signal — new infrastructure usually means dedicated coaching and programme investment follows.

Team structure (gap)

Reed's publishes fixtures and results on sport.reeds.surrey.sch.uk but we haven't yet pulled team counts or fixture volumes into structured data. From the crawl we can see teams labelled 1st XI, 2nd XI, U12A–U15A across cricket and tennis. Ask on tour: "How many pupils represent the school competitively in their main sport in a typical term?"
