Sep – Oct
Research Phase
Start Here — Earlier
Than You Think
🏫
Book school open days now
Popular schools fill open day slots fast. Bangkok Patana, NIST, and Shrewsbury all run September open days — register before the wait list kicks in.
📋
Shortlist to 3 schools maximum
Applications cost time and emotional energy. Shortlist hard — curriculum fit, commute, and budget — before paying any registration fees.
📂
Gather documents early
Passport copies, school reports (last 2 years), immunisation records, and teacher references. These take longer than you expect.

Nov – Dec
Application Window
The Window Opens —
Don't Wait Until January
⚡
Some schools close in November
Oversubscribed schools don't wait for you. Bangkok Patana and NIST can fill Year 7 places by December — submit before Christmas, not after.
💳
Pay the registration fee to hold your spot
Most Bangkok schools charge ฿3,000–฿15,000 non-refundable registration. It reserves a place in the assessment queue, not a guaranteed seat.
✉️
Request teacher references early
Teachers need 2–3 weeks minimum. Ask before the end of term — don't chase them over the holiday break.

Jan – Feb
Assessment Season
What Schools Actually
Test Your Child On
✏️
English & Maths placement tests
Not a pass/fail — schools use these to place your child in the right set. Reassure your child: doing well means appropriate support, not rejection.
👨‍👩‍👧
Parent interview — prepare honestly
Schools ask about your child's learning style, interests, and any additional needs. Be open — hiding a learning need delays the right support by months.
📅
Juggling multiple assessment dates
If you applied to 3 schools, assessments may clash. Contact admissions immediately — most schools will reschedule once within reason.

Mar – Apr
Offers & Decisions
Offer in Hand —
What to Do Next
📬
Offer letter arrives — read it carefully
Check which year group, whether any conditions apply (e.g. English support), and the enrollment deposit deadline — usually 14 days.
⏱️
Deposit deadline is firm
Most deposits are ฿30,000–฿80,000 and non-refundable. Confirm before paying — once you pay for one school, withdrawing from another means losing that fee too.
On the waiting list?
Call — don't email. Ask your position number and how fast it typically moves. Lists move significantly between March and June as families make final decisions.
