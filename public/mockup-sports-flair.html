3 ways to add visual flair to the sports section

Same Reed's data you already have in the report — three different visual treatments. Pick one or mix (e.g. V1 tier cards + V2 competition cards). The data tables that are working well (opponents, team depth) stay unchanged; the variants only touch the sections that feel like "blob of text" today.

V1 · Signature flair V2 · Card-first V3 · Sport-by-sport cards
Variant 1 · "Signature flair"

Minimal change — visual anchors on key blocks, data tables stay

Keeps the rigour of tables for competitions/teams/opponents. Adds colour-coded tier cards, trophy cards for national tournaments, avatar cards for coaches, and icon-grid facilities. Safe, scannable, on-brand.

Which one?

V1 · Signature flair — Safe, keeps everything you have. Just adds tier cards, trophy cards, avatar coaches, icon facilities. ~30 min to build.

V2 · Card-first — Biggest visual impact. Dark hero banner, coloured competition cards, horizontal coach scroll. ~45 min.

V3 · Sport-by-sport cards — Tells the best story per sport. Major redesign of the middle of the section. ~1 hour. (I'd pair this with keeping the opponents + team-depth tables at the bottom for rigour.)

My pick: V1 (low risk, looks meaningfully better) or V1 + parts of V3 (tier cards from V1, sport-by-sport cards for the 3 Major sports only, tables for the rest). Tell me which way.
