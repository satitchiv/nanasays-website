208 reported incidents within a mile over 12 months is at or below the Surrey average for a commuter suburb. The headline "40% violent crime" is misleading without UK context — in police data, "violent crime" includes any unwanted physical contact, from a minor push to serious assault. Roughly 70–80% involve no injury. For families from Bangkok or Singapore, this is not comparable to "violent crime" in those countries and should not alarm.
