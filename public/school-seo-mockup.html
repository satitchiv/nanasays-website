Frequently Asked Questions ✦ NEW
FAQ schema markup means Google shows this directly in search results (featured snippets). AI engines cite it verbatim.
What are Wycombe Abbey's A-Level results?
In 2025, 41% of A-Level grades were A* and 76.2% were A* or A. In 2024, 95% of grades were A*, A or B. The school consistently places in the top tier of UK girls' schools by exam performance.
How many Oxbridge places did Wycombe Abbey get in 2025?
17 Oxbridge acceptances in 2025 — 11 Oxford and 6 Cambridge. One in five pupils received Oxbridge offers. Over one-third of leavers took places at QS World top-10 ranked universities.
What are the fees at Wycombe Abbey?
From 2025/26: £20,500 per term for boarders (£61,500 per year) and £15,600 per term for day pupils (£46,800 per year). Academic scholarships (up to 5% fee reduction) and means-tested bursaries are available.
Is Wycombe Abbey a boarding school?
Yes. Wycombe Abbey is primarily a full boarding school for girls aged 10–19 with capacity for 586 boarders across 11 houses. Year 7 girls start in Junior House before joining one of nine Senior Houses.
What was Wycombe Abbey's ISI inspection result?
The school was inspected by the Independent Schools Inspectorate (ISI) in March 2025 and met every Standard. ISI is the government-approved inspection body for independent schools in England.
What entrance exam does Wycombe Abbey use?
Wycombe Abbey uses the ISEB Common Pre-Test and UKiset as part of its admissions process, alongside its own assessments at 11+, 13+, and 16+ entry points. The application deadline is 1 June each year.

Academic Results block

SEO: Captures high-intent searches like "Wycombe Abbey A level results 2025", "Wycombe Abbey GCSE", "Wycombe Abbey Oxbridge 2025". These terms have real search volume and zero competition from other directories.

AEO: Perplexity/ChatGPT cite factual sentences. "In 2025, 41% of A-Level grades were A*" is exactly the format AI engines pull verbatim.

FAQ Schema

SEO: Google's FAQ rich results show your Q&A directly in SERPs — free real estate below your blue link. Each FAQ answer is 2–3 sentences with the key fact bolded, matching the featured snippet format.

AEO: AI search answers "what entrance exam does Wycombe Abbey use?" by citing you. Without this, it cites the school's own site or nothing.

ISI Inspection Banner

SEO + Trust: "ISI" is a named entity Google understands. Inspection result + year + body name = authoritative signal. Also answers "is Wycombe Abbey safe / good?" intent directly.

Parent Fit Signals

AEO: Conversational AI searches like "is Wycombe Abbey good for a shy girl?" or "Wycombe Abbey vs Cheltenham Ladies" need comparative signals. The fit cards feed those comparisons without giving away the full paid analysis.

Sport Overview

SEO: "Wycombe Abbey netball" / "Wycombe Abbey lacrosse" are long-tail searches from sport-focused parents. 107 teams is a compelling, citable fact that no other directory surfaces.
