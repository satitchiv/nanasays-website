Reed's School

Cobham, Surrey · founded 1813 · ~700 pupils · day + boarding

Quick verdict

Strong all-rounder with notable strength in tennis (LTA RPDC centre). 50%+ A/A* at GCSE. Pastoral system designed around small "houses" with resident staff.

Key facts

Day fees from £8,820/term. Boarding from £14,200/term.

Fees & true cost

Reed's publishes its 2025/26 fee schedule. Most parents add 10-15% in extras.

What are Reed's fees?
Short Answer
Reed's day fees are £8,820/term and full boarding is £14,200/term — about £42,600/year for boarding before extras.
Confirmed Facts
  • Day pupils (Y7–Y13): £8,820/term Fees ↑

A full boarding year at Reed's is £42,600 in tuition — but real "all-in" lands closer to £45-49k once you add uniform, trips, music lessons, and exam fees.

For a day pupil, expect ~£29-32k/year all-in. Reed's sits at the lower-mid end of UK independent boarding costs (compared to ~£42k average per ISC 2024).
