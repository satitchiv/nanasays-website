What are Reed's fees?
Short Answer
Reed's day fees are £8,820 per term and full boarding is £14,200 per term — about £42,600 per year for boarding, before extras.
Confirmed Facts
  • Day pupils (Years 7–13): £8,820 per term Fees
  • Full boarding (Years 9–13): £14,200 per term Fees
  • Registration fee: £150 reeds.surrey/admissions
  • Deposit on offer of a place: £2,000 reeds.surrey/admissions
What This Means

For a day pupil at Reed's, expect roughly £29,000–£32,000 a year all-in once you account for trips, uniforms, music lessons, and exam fees. Full boarding lands closer to £45,000–£49,000 a year.


In context: Reed's sits at the lower-mid end of UK independent boarding costs. The ISC's 2024 average for boarding is approximately £42,000.
