Reed's School

Cobham, Surrey · founded 1813 · ~700 pupils · day + boarding

Quick verdict

Strong all-rounder with notable strength in tennis (LTA RPDC centre, Tennis Academy with 8 coaches). 50%+ A/A* at GCSE, 52% at A-Level. Pastoral system designed around small "houses" with resident staff. Sits at the lower-mid end of UK independent boarding costs.

Key facts

Day fees from £8,820/term. Boarding from £14,200/term. Registration £150, deposit £2,000 on offer. Day-school routine with optional flexi-boarding.

Fees & true cost

Reed's publishes its 2025/26 fee schedule. The headline figures don't include extras like trips, music lessons, exam fees, or uniforms — most parents add 10-15% on top.

Sibling discount applies from the third child onwards (not the second).

Pastoral & wellbeing

Each house has a resident Head of House, matron, and tutor team. The 2025 ISI inspection rated pastoral provision "excellent" with a noted strength in personalised feedback…
